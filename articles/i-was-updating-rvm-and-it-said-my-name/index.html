RVM knows my name

Jun/2011

Today I was updating rvm and it said my name.

And I wondered how it knew?

Was it psychic?

Were they watching me, right now, outside my office window??

No, its just this:

name=$(git config user.name 2>/dev/null)
${name:-"${USER:-$(id | sed -e 's/^[^(]*(//' -e 's/).*$//')}"}

Fun!

My Life with the

Thrill Kill iPad Kult

So I’m going to spend the next week or so with an iPad2 and try to use it wherever I can.

Unfortunately there’s no official Tumblr app for iPad, only the iPhone one, and that is not really adequate. So I’ll see what else there is and try that out.

Of note is that I’ve never had an iTunes/Apple account before. I’m very annoyed that they force you to supply credit card information for it, which is one of the reasons why I never did before.

So far I’ve grabbed some eBooks, a couple of games, watched some stuff on Netflix, and realized how annoying it is to not have a decent chat client, and on top of that having no meaningful multitasking.

We’ll see what the next week holds..
